# Launch Window Marketing

## Positioning
Launch Window is a tiny browser game about shipping under pressure.

Pitch:
> A fast, replayable 7-day startup sprint. Pick your moves, manage runway, and launch before the window closes.

## Core Hook
- 10 to 60 second runs
- easy to understand
- score-chasing and replayable
- startup/product theme with broad internet appeal

## Store Short Description
A fast browser game about building hype, managing runway, and launching on the perfect day.

## Store Long Description
Launch Window is a small, polished browser game where you have seven in-game days to turn an idea into a launch.

Each day you choose one move:
- build
- polish
- hype
- discount
- pivot
- launch

The goal is simple: balance progress, trend fit, runway, and attention to hit the best possible launch score before time runs out.

Designed for quick sessions, instant restarts, and one-more-run energy.
